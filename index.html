WELCOME TO THE IEI LAB

Immersive Experiences

Creating multisensory Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, and Mixed Reality applications, and investigating the impact on human behavior.

Empathic Computing

Researching and developing systems that can recognize, interpret, process, and simulate human affects through Machine Learning.

Human-Computer Interaction

Designing interaces for novel interactions, focusing on social implications such as privacy and cybersickness.

We are a research group in the field of eXtended Reality (VR/MR/AR), and data-driven Empathic Computing, based on the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) perspective. We are particularly interested in the fully personalized immersive and interactive experience to solve real-world problems in education, training, collaboration, simulation, and healthcare, via improving human perception and cognition. Understanding and analyzing the digital domain with a virtual avatar, embodiment, multi-sensory, intuitive user interface system, using both empirical and statistical examination to effectively deliver the immersion and interaction experience is key in our research.

JOINING OUR LAB!

The IEI Lab is actively looking for highly motivated students with a passion for solving challenging research problems in Immersive Reality, Empathic Computing, and Human-Computer Interaction. We are welcoming Ph.D., M.S., and Undergraduate researchers! Ideal applicants should have the following qualifications:

Required:

  • Technical background in Computer Science, Computer Game Design and Development, or a similar discipline;
  • Collaborate well with other team members;
  • Knowledge or experience in at least one of the following areas: 3D Game Engines (Unity 3D or Unreal), the NVIDIA Omniverse Platform, Extended Reality (Virtual Reality or Augmented Reality).

Recommended:

  • Proficiency in one or more of the following programming languages: C++, C#, or Python;
  • Knowledge or experience in at least one of the following disciplines: Human-Computer Interaction, Cognitive Science, Empathic Computing, Computer Graphics or Computer Vision, User Studies.

Students who would like to apply to our lab must submit their resume via **KSU student e-mail** to sjung11@kennesaw.edu.
